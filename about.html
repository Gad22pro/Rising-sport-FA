About Us

Rising Sport FA is a Rwandan football academy founded with
the goal of nurturing young football talent. Led by Coach Enock Kwizera,
we aim to build players who shine both locally and internationally.
Our players train hard, respect the game, and grow as a team.

Rising Sport Football Academy started to work in 2021 with the aim
to become strong football academy the first football pitch were used
located in MUSEZERO cell .

Started a low number of players like 10 players but now we have a big
number of players in different categories like U7,U10,U13,U15 and U17 .
